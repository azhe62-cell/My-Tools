Lorem Ipsum: What It Is and Why We Still Use It

June 12, 2026 6 min read

If you've spent any time around web design or print layouts, you've seen blocks of Latin-looking text that don't quite make sense — "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit..." This text has been a staple of design mockups for over 500 years. Here's the story behind it and why it's still useful today.

What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text used to fill design layouts before real content is ready. It's derived from a scrambled passage of Latin text from Cicero's work "De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" (On the Ends of Good and Evil), written around 45 BC.

The text has been altered over centuries — words are added, removed, and rearranged — to the point where it's no longer grammatically correct or meaningful Latin. It simply resembles real text in terms of letter frequency, word length, and overall structure.

The History of Lorem Ipsum

The most widely repeated origin story traces Lorem Ipsum back to the 1500s, when an unknown printer used it to create a type specimen book — a sample sheet showing off different fonts and layouts. Because the text needed to look like real writing without being distracting (readers might get pulled into reading actual content rather than evaluating the typeface), scrambled Latin was perfect.

The text was then "rediscovered" and popularized in the 1960s when it was used in Letraset sheets (adhesive lettering used by graphic designers before digital design existed). From there, it made its way into early desktop publishing software like Aldus PageMaker, cementing its place as the default placeholder text for generations of designers.

Why Designers Use Placeholder Text at All

When building a layout — whether a website, app, brochure, or presentation — designers need to know how text will actually look and behave within the design before the final content is written. Placeholder text serves several purposes:

  • Testing layout and spacing — See how a paragraph of realistic length fits within a column or card
  • Evaluating typography — Check how a font looks at different sizes and weights with body text, not just headlines
  • Avoiding distraction — Real, meaningful text pulls a reviewer's attention to the content rather than the design itself — exactly the problem Lorem Ipsum was originally created to solve
  • Working in parallel — Designers can build layouts while content writers are still working on the actual copy
  • Demonstrating to clients — Show a polished-looking mockup without needing final, approved copy

Why Not Just Use Real Text?

You might wonder why designers don't just use real sentences in English. The answer comes back to the "distraction" problem — if the placeholder text is meaningful, reviewers naturally start reading and evaluating the content, commenting on word choice or making editorial suggestions, when the actual purpose of the review is to evaluate the visual design.

Lorem Ipsum's familiar-but-meaningless nature signals clearly: "this is placeholder, focus on the layout, not the words."

Modern Alternatives to Lorem Ipsum

While Lorem Ipsum remains the standard, some designers use alternatives for specific contexts — like generating placeholder text in other languages for testing internationalization, or using contextual filler that matches the tone of the final content (for example, generating placeholder product names for an e-commerce mockup). However, generic Lorem Ipsum remains the fastest and most universally recognized choice for general layout work.

When You Need Different Amounts of Text

Different parts of a layout need different amounts of placeholder text — a heading might need just a few words, a paragraph needs several sentences, and a full article mockup needs multiple paragraphs.
