Security & Privacy Protocol

Last synchronized: April 14, 2026

1. Data Processing Architecture

"My Tools" implements a strictly decentralized execution environment. Utility computations (such as QR generation, Morse cryptography, and raw text insights) are executed exclusively via your device's browser core. We do not extract, store, or warehouse input variables sent to the local tools. Your data does not leave your physical machine during execution.

2. Cookies and Third-Party Advertising Framework

To maintain free global access without recurring user subscriptions, we utilize third-party advertising partners, principally Google AdSense, to serve relevant digital placements on our UI layer.

These systems establish algorithmic cookies—identifiers stored within your browser—to facilitate targeted distributions corresponding precisely to previous browsing indices established on this domain or external web hubs.

3. Consent & Analytics Verification

By initializing sessions on our tools suite, you confirm adherence to these passive infrastructure trackers unless disabled explicitly via your core browser settings. Anonymous analytical tracking may be utilized solely for bandwidth optimization and user experience enhancement reviews.
