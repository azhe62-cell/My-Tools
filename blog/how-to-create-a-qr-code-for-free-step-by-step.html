How to create a QR Code for free (step-by-step)

April 14, 2026 4 min read

Need to link physical customers to your website instantly? Creating a QR code is faster and easier than ever, and you do not need an expensive service to do it.

1. Step 1: Choose Your Data

Decide what you want your audience to see when they scan the code. This could be the direct URL to your homepage, an exclusive discount link, a YouTube video, or even just some raw text for a secret message.

2. Step 2: Use a Reliable Generator

Instead of risking your data on a third-party paid tool, use a local browser-side utility. An in-browser generator ensures your link is instantly turned into a matrix code locally, without storing your data on a random server.

3. Step 3: Download and Deploy

Once your code is generated, ensure you download a high-quality PNG. It should have a clean white background with a dark contrast core for maximal scannability. Now, you can place this image on your business card, flyer, or digital stream.
